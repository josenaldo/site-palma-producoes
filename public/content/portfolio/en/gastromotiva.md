## Video

In partnership with CallBMídia, we conducted Video Capture, Direction, and Editing for the Christmas Campaign featuring a message from David Hertz, Founder of Gastromotiva - a non-governmental organization that uses gastronomy as a driver for social transformation. Since 2006, they have been involved in initiatives to combat hunger, food insecurity, and income generation through education.
